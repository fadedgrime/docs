## Purpose
The purpose of this documentation center is to provide resources for customers, the community, and staff. Its primary goal is to help our members find assistance without needing to open support tickets. If you can't find what you're looking for, please feel free to open a ticket.

## Look around
Use the official Fare Dollar links on the left side panel, or feel free to explore our documentation. See you around!
