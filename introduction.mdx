## About Fare Dollar
Fare Dollar is one of thousands of grocery retail groups striving for customer satisfaction with low, affordable prices. From household utilities to fresh fruits and vegetables, we ensure we have all the essentials for a happy life. Our associates make Fare Dollar a fun, awesome, and memorable experience for our customers.

<Card title="Fun fact icon="seedling" iconType="duotone" color="#ca8b04">
  Fare Dollar was formerly known as Marketly in 2023. Due to in-game attacks by hired developers, Marketly had to shut down. In 2024, we rebranded as Fare Dollar, focusing on making our prices and products more affordable while maintaining high quality.
</Card>
