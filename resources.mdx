### Mental Health

<Note>Fare Dollar employees are not equipped to assist with mental health issues. Please utilize the resources below for support. Remember, you are not alone.</Note>

<Tip>If you cannot access the resources below, consider reaching out to 988. You can call 988 directly from your phone, or chat online at 988lifeline.org.</Tip>

<Warning>If you are in the process of a suicide attempt, please call 911 immediately or visit your local emergency department.</Warning>
